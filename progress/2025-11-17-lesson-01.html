Lesson #1 – 2025-11-17

Main topic: Present Perfect vs Past Simple • Type: Grammar & Expression

What we did (contenu de la leçon) :

  • Spoke about past weekends and first days in Australia.
  • Reviewed difference between I have done and I did.
  • Practised sentences with for, since, ever, never.

Strengths (points forts) :

  • Good fluency when talking about everyday life.
  • Good basic vocabulary for daily situations in Australia.

Weak points / to improve (points faibles) :

  • Confusion with time expressions: for / since / ago.
  • Sometimes uses Past Simple instead of Present Perfect for life experiences.

Next time / Homework ideas :

  • Review irregular past participles (been, gone, done, written...).
  • Write a short paragraph about “How my life has changed since I arrived in Australia”.

Linked grammar sheets:

  • Present Perfect – experiences

General feeling: good motivation, needs more automaticity with verb tenses and time expressions.
