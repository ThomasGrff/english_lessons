Present Perfect – experiences

Level: B1–B2 • Tags: Tenses Speaking Life in Australia

Form: have / has + past participle

Main uses:

  • Life experiences (no specific time): I have visited Sydney three times.
  • Recent past with result now: I’ve just finished my homework.
  • Actions that started in the past and continue now: I have lived in Brisbane for 2 months.

Time expressions / signal words:

  • ever, never, already, yet, just, recently, for, since

Common mistakes (à surveiller) :

  • ❌ I am here since two months. → ✅ I’ve been here for two months.
  • ❌ I have gone to Australia last year. → ✅ I went to Australia last year. (Past Simple avec temps précis)

Personal notes:

I often confuse Present Perfect and Past Simple. I need to think about: “Is the time finished (last year, yesterday)? → Past Simple. Is it about my life in general or a result now? → Present Perfect.”
