Present Perfect vs Past Simple

Level: B2

1. Form

Present Perfect: Subject + have/has + past participle

Past Simple: Subject + verb-ed / irregular form

2. Main Uses

a) Present Perfect: connection to now

  • Life experience: "I have tried surfing."
  • Duration until now: "She has lived here for three years."

b) Past Simple: finished time

  • Completed action in a finished time: "I went to Brisbane last month."

3. Keywords

  • Present Perfect: ever, never, already, yet, for, since
  • Past Simple: yesterday, last week, in 2020, two years ago

4. Contrast

Main difference: Use Past Simple for finished time, Present Perfect for actions connected to the present.

5. Useful Expressions

  • Have you ever...? – Pour demander une expérience.
  • I’ve lived here since...
  • I went there last...
